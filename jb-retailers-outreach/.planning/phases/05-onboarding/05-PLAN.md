# PHASE 05 PLAN: Onboarding — Wow Them in Week 1

## Objective
Set up the full technical foundation and deliver visible results within 7 days of contract signing. First impressions are everything — Ali Khan needs to feel like he made the right decision immediately.

## The Onboarding Principle
> Every day without visible progress is a day Ali Khan second-guesses his decision.
> Ship something every 24 hours for the first 7 days.

---

## Day 1 — Kickoff Meeting & Asset Collection (2 hours)

**Run via WhatsApp video or in-person. Collect:**

### Assets Needed from Ali Khan:
- [ ] Logo file (JPG/PNG/PDF — any format)
- [ ] Brand colors (ask him to send 2–3 product photos he likes — extract colors from those)
- [ ] Product photos (ask him to send 20–30 photos of best-selling items via WhatsApp)
- [ ] Price list (rough prices by category — kurtas, perfumes, accessories)
- [ ] Business phone number for WhatsApp Business
- [ ] Business email (if none, create jbretailers@gmail.com or jbretailers.business@gmail.com)
- [ ] UPI/payment details for website payment gateway

### Questions to Confirm in Kickoff:
1. "Kaunse 5 products sabse zyada bik te hain? Woh pehle website pe daalte hain"
2. "Aapka delivery zone kya hoga? Chandigarh only? Tricity? All India?"
3. "Cash on delivery chahiye ya online payment only?"
4. "Aapka Instagram handle confirm: @jbretailers — is it the main account?"
5. "Ek aadmi hai jo WhatsApp orders ka reply karega? Ya hum handle karein?"

## Day 1–2 — Domain + Tech Setup

### Domain Purchase:
- Register `jbretailers.in` (₹500–800/year via GoDaddy / Namecheap)
- Fallback: `jbretailerschandigarh.in` or `shopjbretailers.in`

### Shopify Setup:
- Create Shopify store (Basic plan — ₹1,994/month billed to client OR Starter ₹799)
- Connect domain
- Install theme: Dawn (free, clean, fast) or Debut
- Brand colors + logo applied
- Homepage structure: Hero banner → New Arrivals → Categories (Kurtas / Perfumes / Accessories) → Instagram Feed → WhatsApp CTA

### WhatsApp Business API (WATI):
- Sign up at wati.io (₹2,999/month plan)
- Connect Ali Khan's business number
- Set up:
  - Auto-reply: "Shukriya! Aapka message mila. Hum jald jawab denge 🙏"
  - Welcome message with store link
  - Business hours message (outside 10 AM–9:30 PM)
  - Product catalog (WhatsApp native catalog — upload top 20 products)

## Day 2–3 — First 10 Products Live

### Product Listing Process (AI-Powered):
For each product photo Ali Khan sends:
1. **Name the product:** "Men's Embroidered Pathani Kurta — Off White"
2. **Write description (Claude AI):** 3-sentence description + fabric details + styling tip
3. **Set price + variants** (size: S/M/L/XL/XXL, color if applicable)
4. **Add SEO tags:** "men kurta chandigarh", "pathani suit mohali", "wedding kurta punjab"
5. **Upload to Shopify**

**Target: 10 products live by Day 3, 50 products by Day 14**

## Day 3 — Instagram Automation Goes Live

4. Send Ali Khan screenshot: "Kal subah 8 baje se automated posts shuru hogi — 3/day, aapko kuch nahi karna"
