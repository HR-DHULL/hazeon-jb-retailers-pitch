# PHASE 02 PLAN: First Contact — Show Up With Proof

## Objective
Make first contact with Ali Khan via Instagram DM + in-person store visit. Lead with the work already done, not a sales pitch.

## Core Principle (from Cold Email Skill)
> "Lead with their world, not yours. The reader should see their own situation reflected back."

Ali Khan doesn't care about Hazeon. He cares about his business. Every message leads with what we found about HIS store and what we already fixed.

---

## Channel Strategy (Priority Order)

### Channel 1: Instagram DM @jbretailers (PRIMARY — Day 3)

**Message 1 — The Hook (send with screenshot proof):**
```
Bhai, aapka TripAdvisor listing unclaimed pada tha — aur wrong category mein tha (Attractions, not Shopping).
Maine claim kar liya aur fix kar diya. ✅

JB Retailers ka digital setup dekha — bahut scope hai.
15 minute milenge kabhi? Kuch interesting cheezein dikhani hain.

— [Your Name], Hazeon
```

*(Attach: TripAdvisor screenshot showing claimed listing)*

**Why Hinglish?** Ali Khan's store is in Chandigarh. His Instagram audience is Tricity. Hinglish builds instant rapport — makes it feel personal, not corporate.

**Best time to visit:** Tuesday–Thursday, 2–5 PM (post-lunch, pre-evening rush)
**Location:** SCO 77, Ground Floor, Sector 15D, Chandigarh (Near Gopal Sweets)

**Script for walk-in:**
```
"Bhai, main Harsh hoon — Hazeon se. We help local Tricity businesses grow online.

Aapka store dekha Instagram pe — 32K followers, mast content. But noticed aapka
website kaam nahi kar raha. Maine aur hamare team ne kuch kaam kar diya tha
aapke liye — dikhaunga? 5 minute?"

[Open laptop/phone — show the 5 quick wins already built]

```

**One-pager to leave behind:** Print the competitor audit table + one line: *"Your 32K followers are worth ₹50+ Lakh/year online. Call us to find out how."*

| Day 8 | In-person visit | Walk-in with laptop + printed one-pager |

| Day 14 | Final DM | "Closing the loop" message — no hard sell |

```

Jo kaam kiya tha — TripAdvisor, GMB, content calendar — woh sab aapka hi hai.
Koi charge nahi.

Agar kabhi digital presence build karni ho toh hamare paas aa jayein.
```

*(This "generosity without pressure" close often gets a reply within 24 hours.)*

---

## What NOT to Do
- Do NOT pitch pricing in first contact
- Do NOT send a long proposal PDF upfront
- Do NOT use corporate language ("leverage synergies", "end-to-end solutions")
- Do NOT send 3+ messages in one day
- Do NOT follow up more than 6 times total

## Verification Criteria (Phase Complete When:)
- [ ] Instagram DM sent with TripAdvisor screenshot
- [ ] At least 1 follow-up sent
- [ ] In-person store visit attempted
- [ ] Positive response received OR phone number obtained
- [ ] Meeting / call scheduled
