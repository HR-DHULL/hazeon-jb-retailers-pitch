# PHASE 04 PLAN: Proposal & Close — Make It Impossible to Say No

## Objective
Present a customized proposal, handle every objection, and get a signed contract + 50% advance in one meeting.

## Proposal Meeting Format
**Format:** In-person (bring laptop — show, don't tell)
**Duration:** 45–60 minutes
**Bring:** Laptop with live demos, printed one-pager summary, contract ready to send

---

## Presentation Flow (The 5-Step Close)

### Step 1: Reframe the Problem (5 minutes)
Open by reflecting back what he told you in discovery:

```
"Ali bhai, last time aapne kaha tha [his exact words from discovery].
Mujhe yeh bahut important laga. Aur isliye main aaj exactly usi
problem ka solution lekar aaya hoon."
```

**Key reframe:** Don't say "we offer digital marketing." Say:
> "Aapka ek invisible 4th store banana chahte hain — jo 24 ghante kaam kare,
> teeno cities mein serve kare, aur jab bhi koi Instagram pe kurta dekhe —
> Buy Now button ho."

---

### Step 2: Show the Vision (10 minutes)
Show a live Shopify store demo (use a template store as example). Walk through:
1. "Yeh product page hai — customer ne Instagram pe dekha, directly yahan aaya, order diya"
2. "Yeh WhatsApp integration — order aaya, aapko WhatsApp pe notification"
3. "Yeh analytics — aap dekh sakte ho kitne orders aye, kahan se aaye, kitna revenue"

**Powerline:** *"Aap so rahe the raat mein, aur 3 orders aa gaye. Yahi banana chahte hain."*

---

### Step 3: Present the Package (10 minutes)

**Always start with Growth Package. Never start with Starter.**

```
GROWTH PACKAGE — What We Build Together

Setup (One-Time): ₹1,80,000
Monthly: ₹35,000/month

What you get:
✅ Full e-commerce website (Shopify) — your own jbretailers.in
✅ 50+ products listed with AI-written descriptions
✅ WhatsApp Business automation (order notifications, customer support)
✅ 3 Instagram posts/day — automated, no effort from your side
✅ Google My Business — all 3 stores optimized
✅ CRM — every customer tracked, birthday offers, loyalty points
✅ Meta Ads — ₹10,000/month ad budget management (ad spend extra)
✅ Monthly analytics report — revenue, traffic, customer data
✅ Direct WhatsApp support from our team

Timeline: Live in 3 weeks.
```

**Then show the ROI math:**
```
Setup: ₹1,80,000 (one-time)
Monthly: ₹35,000

If only 50 customers/month order online @ avg ₹2,500/order:
Monthly online revenue = ₹1,25,000
Your cost = ₹35,000
Net gain = ₹90,000/month

Break even: Month 2.
Year 1 online revenue: ₹10–15 Lakh (conservative).
```

---

### Step 4: Handle Objections (Have These Ready)

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| **"Bahut expensive hai"** | "Bhai, ek mahine mein 50 extra orders aayenge toh ₹1.25 Lakh milenge. Cost ₹35K hai. Yeh expense nahi, investment hai." |
| **"Pehle chhota start karte hain"** | "Bilkul — Starter Package hai ₹75K setup + ₹15K/month. Website + WhatsApp + GMB. Start karte hain aur baad mein expand karte hain." |
| **"Mujhe nahi pata digital kaam karega mera business ke liye"** | "Isiliye first month mein ek festival campaign chalate hain — Eid sale. Aap khud results dekhna. Agar ₹1 Lakh nahi aaya, main paise wapas kar dunga." (Performance guarantee if confident) |
| **"Mere paas time nahi yeh sab manage karne ka"** | "Aapko kuch nahi karna. Hum manage karte hain. Aapko sirf WhatsApp pe orders dekhne hain." |
| **"Pehle kisi ne kiya tha, kaam nahi aaya"** | "Kya hua tha exactly? [Listen] Yeh kya galat kiya woh — [address specifically]. Humara approach alag hai kyunki [specific difference]." |
| **"Agency pe trust nahi"** | "Isiliye pehle free kaam kiya. TripAdvisor, GMB — kuch bhi charge nahi kiya. Hum results dikhate hain pehle, charge baad mein." |
| **"Sochna padega"** | "Bilkul — kya specifically unclear hai? Timeline? Pricing? Jo bhi hai, abhi clear karte hain." |

---

### Step 5: Close (The Ask)

**The exact close:**
```
"Ali bhai, ek kaam karte hain — aaj sign karo, 50% advance do,
aur hum kal se kaam shuru karte hain. 3 hafte mein website live.

Pehla mahina mein performance guarantee:
Agar website pe ₹50,000 ke orders nahi aaye pehle 30 din mein —
toh main poora advance refund kar dunga.

Deal?"
```

*(Performance guarantee removes all risk. Only offer this if you're confident — which you should be.)*

## If He Says No Today
```
"Koi baat nahi Ali bhai. Jo kaam pehle kiya tha — TripAdvisor, GMB, content
calendar — woh sab yours hai. Koi charge nahi.

Jab bhi ready ho, hum hain. Mera number save kar lo."
```
Then follow up once in 2 weeks, once in 1 month. Don't ghost and don't harass.
