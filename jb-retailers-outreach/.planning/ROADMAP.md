## Phase 1: Pre-Outreach Prep `[Days 1–3]`
**Theme: Do the work before asking for anything**
Build 5 quick wins to deliver for FREE before ever asking for a meeting.
- [ ] Claim JB Retailers TripAdvisor listing
- [ ] Fix Google My Business (3 store addresses, photos, correct URL)
- [ ] Build link-in-bio page (Linktree-style) with catalog + WhatsApp CTA
- [ ] Generate 30-day Instagram content calendar
- [ ] Create competitor digital audit (Raymond vs Manyavar vs JB Retailers)

**Goal must be TRUE:** Hazeon has already created tangible value for JB Retailers before any conversation starts.

---

## Phase 2: First Contact `[Days 3–7]`
**Theme: Be the person who already helped them**
Reach out across multiple channels with proof of work already done.
- [ ] Send Instagram DM to @jbretailers (primary channel)
- [ ] Draft WhatsApp message (get number from store visit or JustDial)
- [ ] Visit Sector 15D store in person — meet Ali Khan or store manager
- [ ] Follow-up sequence (3-touch over 7 days if no response)

**Goal must be TRUE:** Ali Khan has seen/received the proof-of-work and responded positively.

## Phase 3: Discovery Meeting `[Days 7–14]`
**Theme: Listen 80%, talk 20%**
In-person or video meeting to understand his goals, fears, and priorities.
- [ ] Schedule meeting (in-store or WhatsApp video call)
- [ ] Run discovery questions (BANT + pain-based questions)
- [ ] Present the quick wins already delivered
- [ ] Show the competitor gap analysis live
- [ ] Understand his budget mindset (what he's already spending on ads/digital)
- [ ] Identify his #1 fear and #1 desire

**Goal must be TRUE:** You know exactly what Ali Khan wants most and what would stop him from saying yes.

## Phase 4: Proposal & Close `[Days 14–21]`
**Theme: Make it impossible to say no**
Deliver the tailored proposal and close on Growth Package.
- [ ] Customize the pitch document for exactly what he said in discovery
- [ ] Present in-person (not email — show up with a laptop)
- [ ] Start with Growth Package (₹1.8L setup + ₹35K/month)
- [ ] Have Starter Package as fallback (₹75K + ₹15K)

**Goal must be TRUE:** Signed contract + 50% advance payment received.

## Phase 5: Onboarding `[Week 3–4]`
**Theme: Wow them in the first week**

## Phase 6: Full Execution `[Month 1–3]`
**Theme: Make the numbers undeniable**
Deliver results that make Ali Khan's decision look genius — and set up upsell to Premium.
- [ ] 50+ products live on Shopify with AI-written descriptions

- [ ] Google Maps ranking tracked weekly

- [ ] Month 3 review meeting — present ROI report, propose Premium upsell

**Goal must be TRUE:** ₹5 Lakh+ in tracked online revenue. Ali Khan tells 2 other business owners about Hazeon.
